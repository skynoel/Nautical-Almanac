• Astron holds data for two CUSTOM STARS.
• On changing this option to "ONE" or "TWO", the CUSTOM STAR(S) become visible and the upper star (ONE) or lower star (TWO) becomes the currently selected body. Other bodies can be selected as normal.
• On all other pages, the CUSTOM STAR names are preceeded by a "★" distinguishing mark.
• The CUSTOM STARS are shown as the last two bodies in the body select list and elsewhere. [Keystroke hint: Press "z" then "⯆"]
• When option set to "ONE" or "TWO", the CUSTOM STAR(S) are included in all Astron utilities.
• When option set to "NONE", the stars are excluded and this table hidden.
• When Astron is closed, CUSTOM STARS data are remembered for next use.
• On first use, the CUSTOM STARS are set to Castor (α Geminorum) and Mirach (β Andromedae).
• When entering values, RA and Dec must be in degrees and decimal degrees.
• pm RA values are in milliarcseconds of ARC, not mas of Right Ascension.
